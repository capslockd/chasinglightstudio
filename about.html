About

Hello, and welcome to Chasing Light Studio.

I'm a photographer who believes the best photos happen when people forget the camera is there — in the middle of a laugh, a quiet glance, or the chaos of a birthday celebration. My style is warm, natural and unposed, always chasing the best light in the room.

Whether it's a themed birthday party, a family gathering or a milestone worth remembering, I'd love to help you hold onto it.
